# Imperflora

## Project Overview

Imperflora is a flower sales application that combines e-commerce with social networking features. Users can browse through a vast selection of flowers, plants, and related products, share their experiences, create profiles, and connect with other flower enthusiasts.

## Features
- **Flower Catalog:** View a comprehensive catalog of flowers and plants with detailed descriptions and images.
- **Shopping Cart:** Easily add items to your cart and proceed to checkout.
- **User Profiles:** Create a personalized profile to showcase your favorite flowers and arrangements.
- **Social Feed:** Share updates, photos, and experiences with friends and followers.
- **Ratings and Reviews:** Rate and review products to help the community make informed decisions.

- **Backend:** Node.js with Express for server-side logic
- **Database:** MongoDB for data storage
